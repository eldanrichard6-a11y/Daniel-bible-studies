Chapter I

Foundations of Divine Love

The journey of faith begins not with rules, but with a revelation—the awe-inspiring realization of God's unconditional love. This is a love that doesn't just observe us from afar; it pursues us, heals us, and restores us through the person of Jesus Christ.


We are called to live not by the superficial demands of the world, but by the eternal principles of the Kingdom of God. Our faith is a living, active force that guides our decisions, shapes our character, and fills us with enduring hope, even in the darkest valleys.

Pillars of the Gospel

REPENTANCE

Turning away from old paths and embracing the new life.

FAITH

Total trust in the invisible, yet all-powerful promise of God.

GRACE

Receiving the free gift of salvation that we cannot earn.

"I am the light of the world. Whoever follows me will not walk in darkness, but will have the light of life."

Chapter II

Living the Kingdom Life Daily

The Gospel is not meant to be confined to Sundays or limited to intellectual assent. It is a transformative lifestyle. Jesus taught us that the greatest commandments are to love God with all our heart and to love our neighbors as ourselves. This is the simple, yet profound, essence of Christian living.


How do we practice sacrificial love in a world driven by self-interest? By serving the broken, by forgiving the offenders, and by being peacemakers in a divided world. Let your light shine, that others may see your good works and glorify your Father in heaven.
